# 6.1 Gallery Page (SSR)

**Phase:** 6 — Gallery & Polish

**Goal:** Filterable, paginated gallery page with category filters and links to related content.

## User Stories

- As a visitor, I want to browse a curated gallery of photos.
- As a visitor, I want to filter gallery photos by category.
- As a visitor, I want to click a photo to visit the related bear or product.
