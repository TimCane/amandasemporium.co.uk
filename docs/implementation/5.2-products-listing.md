# 5.2 Products Listing Page (SSR)

**Phase:** 5 — Products & Events

**Goal:** Filterable, paginated products catalogue with category filters.

## User Stories

- As a potential customer, I want to browse products by category.
- As a visitor, I want to see what things cost before visiting a market.
- As a visitor, I want to see photos of the handmade products.
