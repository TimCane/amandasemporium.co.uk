We found Abigail at a car boot sale in Royal Tunbridge Wells {RESCUED_DATE_AGO}. Cream and white fur, soft and fluffy, with button eyes and a neatly embroidered nose.

She needed a proper outfit. We ran her up a little reversible dress, pink on one side with a heart print, and stitched a matching bag to go with it. Pink shorts underneath for good measure.

She came to the Surbiton Festival with us {REHOMED_DATE_AGO} and found her new family there. Someone spotted her on the stall, loved the dress, and that was that.

We hope she's still getting plenty of cuddles.
