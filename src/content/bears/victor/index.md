We found Victor at a car boot sale in Canterbury {RESCUED_DATE_AGO} ago. A Build-A-Bear with golden fur, soft and fluffy, button eyes and a little embroidered nose. He'd clearly been well-loved.

He came to us with no clothes, as they all do. We picked out a short-sleeve floral shirt in blue and white for him. Bottom half left bare, which felt right for a bear with his easygoing energy.

Victor came along to the Surbiton Festival last September. That's where his new person spotted him. He went home with them to Hinchley Wood that same day, {REHOMED_DATE_AGO} ago now.

A good ending for a good bear.
