Avery came to us from a Shepperton car boot {RESCUED_DATE_AGO}. A 2016 Build-a-Bear with rainbow leopard-print fur, sparkly eyes and a printed face. You don't see many like her.

Amanda picked out a purple top and leopard-print shorts for her, plus a little crossbody bag so she had somewhere to keep her things. The love slogan on the top felt right for a bear with that much personality.

She went to the Thatcham Family Fun Day {REHOMED_DATE_AGO} and didn't come home with us. A little girl spotted her, and that was that. Exactly the kind of home we hope for.
