We found Amelia in Addlestone {RESCUED_DATE_AGO}. Golden fur, cream paws, and the sweet embroidered nose you get on the Glorious Britain jointed bears.

Her button eyes still had all their shine. She'd been well-loved, you could tell, but someone had looked after her properly before she came to us.

She needed an outfit. We picked out a little purple dress, hand crocheted, stitch by stitch at the kitchen table. It suited her golden fur beautifully.

Amelia came with us to the Burnham Donkey Derby and that's where she found her new family. Rehomed {REHOMED_DATE_AGO}, dress and all.
