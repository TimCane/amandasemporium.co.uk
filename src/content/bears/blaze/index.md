Blaze is a Build-a-Bear Limited Edition Cool Cat, a ginger tabby from the 2011 range, and he came to us dressed head to paw in full fire fighter gear. We picked him up in Littlehampton {RESCUED_DATE_AGO} ago. His fur was a bit matted and his helmet had seen better days, but you could tell he'd been on active duty for years. We gave him a good brush up and he made his public debut at Surbiton Festival {REHOMED_DATE_AGO} ago, where he found a new home in Tolworth almost immediately. Something about a cat in uniform, people can't resist.
