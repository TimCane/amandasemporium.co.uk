We found Audrey at a car boot sale in Hunston {RESCUED_DATE_AGO}. She's a With Love bear, cream and curly all over, with button eyes and a neat embroidered nose. There's a small heart stitched onto her chest.

She needed a proper outfit. We picked out a red polka-dot dress for her, and a pink bow on a headband to sit in her curls. She looked the part straight away.

Audrey came along to the Surbiton Festival {REHOMED_DATE_AGO}. A little old lady spotted her on the stall and that was that. They left together, Audrey tucked under her arm like she'd always belonged there.
