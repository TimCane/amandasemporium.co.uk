We found Wilf {RESCUED_DATE_AGO} at a car boot sale in Cobham. He's a Bear Factory hippo, solid and squat, with a determined little face. His stitching was good but he had that slightly grubby look that comes from sitting in a bag for too long. A good wash and he came up a treat. Wilf sat on our stall through quite a few markets before finding his person. He was finally rehomed {REHOMED_DATE_AGO} at the Royal British Legion VE/VJ Day Fete in Farnham. Sometimes the right match just takes a while.
