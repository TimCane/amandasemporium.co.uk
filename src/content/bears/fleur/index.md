Fleur came from a car boot sale in Sutton Green {RESCUED_DATE_AGO}. She's a Build-A-Bear flower butterfly bear from 2012, pink and purple with little flowers printed across her fur.

Her muzzle is white and her nose is embroidered, her mouth a neat line of stitches. The button eyes had held up well. She'd clearly been a favourite once.

We dressed her in a pink star-print top and a gold sequin tutu, which felt right for a bear with her sort of sparkle.

She found her new person at the Surbiton Festival {REHOMED_DATE_AGO}, and went home to Surbiton that same afternoon.
