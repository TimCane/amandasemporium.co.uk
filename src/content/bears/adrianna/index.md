Adrianna came to us from Appledram {RESCUED_DATE_AGO}. Cream fur, soft as anything, with a little embroidered nose and a stitched mouth that looks almost shy.

We dressed her in a Build-A-Bear Rapunzel dress we'd been saving for the right bear. Purple bodice, pink sleeves, the full princess cut. Added a tiara and a small cameo brooch at her throat.

Once she was dressed, she looked ready to hold court. We took her to the Guildford Christmas Market {REHOMED_DATE_AGO}, and she didn't stay long. A family from Effingham spotted her across the stall and that was that.

We like to think she's somewhere warm now, tiara slightly askew, being read bedtime stories.
