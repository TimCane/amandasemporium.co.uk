Ash came to us from a table at a Long Parish sale {RESCUED_DATE_AGO}. He'd clearly been well-loved. The fur on his paw pads had gone a bit thin, and his embroidered nose had lost a stitch or two at one corner.

He's a fluffy brown thing with a yellow stripe down his back and a stitched mouth that sits somewhere between a smile and a think. No tag, no brand, no clues. Just a soft seated bear waiting to be picked up.

I knitted him a onesie by hand. Cream wool, a little cardigan shape at the top. It took me a few evenings and I changed the pattern twice. He looked right in it straight away.

We took Ash to the St George's Day event in Horley {REHOMED_DATE_AGO}, and he went home that same afternoon with a lovely family who spotted him first thing.
