We had our usual spot on the high street for Surbiton Festival, set up from noon till five.

Dry to start, a bit chilly. The kind of day where you keep your hands wrapped round a takeaway cup between customers.

My husband made the very first sale of the day, which he won't let me forget any time soon.

Twelve hats went. Twelve! We brought them along almost as an afterthought and they shifted faster than anything else on the table.

Seven bears found new homes too. A proper good day, and a reminder that Surbiton always looks after us.
