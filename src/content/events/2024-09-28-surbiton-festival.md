## Recap

The 2024 Surbiton Festival turned out to be one of the best of the year. The sun came out just as we were setting up and stayed all afternoon. Claremont Gardens was packed with families and the atmosphere was warm and relaxed — exactly the kind of event our bears love.

Four bears found new homes over the course of the afternoon, including one who had been with us since the previous summer. We also sold out of bookmarks and key fobs before 3pm, which has never happened at this event before.

The organisers were friendly, the pitch position was great, and we'll definitely be back in 2025.
