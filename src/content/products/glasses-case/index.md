Fed up with boring black glasses cases? These are padded, pretty, and actually do the job properly. The quilting fabric comes in all sorts of prints, so whether you're after florals, geometrics, or something a bit quirky, there'll be one that catches your eye. The wadding inside gives proper cushioning, and the snap fastener keeps your glasses from sliding out in your bag.

## How It's Made

Each case is cut from quilting cotton with a layer of wadding sandwiched between the outer fabric and a soft cotton lining. The layers are stitched together, turned right-side out, and top-stitched around the opening for a neat edge. A press-stud snap is fitted at the flap so it closes securely. The padding is thick enough to protect your lenses but slim enough to slip into a pocket or handbag.
