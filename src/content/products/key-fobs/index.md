These key fobs are one of those little things that make daily life a bit more cheerful. Made from vibrant quilting cotton wrapped around sturdy hardware, they're easy to spot in the depths of a handbag and handy for telling your house key from your car key at a glance. They make brilliant stocking fillers or just a little treat for yourself.

## How It's Made

A strip of quilting fabric is interfaced for stiffness, pressed in half lengthways, then folded around the key fob hardware. The raw edges are tucked inside and the whole length is top-stitched on both sides for a neat, durable finish. The hardware is clamped on using a press tool to make sure it's properly secure. Quick to make, but done properly every time.
