Is there anything sweeter than a baby in a hand-knitted hat? These come in all sorts of styles — from simple ribbed beanies to crocheted numbers with little ears or flowers — and in enough colours to match any outfit. They're soft against little heads, stretchy enough to stay put, and machine-washable because, well, babies.

## How It's Made

Some are knitted on double-pointed needles for a seamless crown, and others are crocheted in the round — it depends on the style. The yarn is always soft double knit acrylic, chosen because it's gentle on skin, holds its shape, and survives the washing machine. Finishing touches like pom-poms, ears, or flower appliques are stitched on securely by hand.
