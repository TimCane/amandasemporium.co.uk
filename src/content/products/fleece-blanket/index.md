These fleece blankets are wonderfully cuddly and just the right weight for keeping little ones (or big ones, honestly) warm on the sofa or in the pushchair. What makes them special is the hand-crocheted border — it gives a soft, decorative edge that turns a simple fleece into something you'd be proud to give as a gift. At around 100 by 80 centimetres, they're a generous size without being unwieldy.

## How It's Made

The fleece is cut to size and the edges are carefully blanket-stitched by hand all the way around. This line of blanket stitches creates the anchor points for the crocheted border, which is then worked in double knit yarn in a complementary colour. The crochet adds just enough weight to help the blanket drape nicely, and gives it that lovely handmade finish you can spot from across the room.
