These fleece blankets are properly soft and just the right weight for keeping little ones (or big ones, honestly) warm on the sofa or in the pushchair. The hand-crocheted border is what sets them apart — it gives a soft, decorative edge that turns a simple fleece into something you'd be happy to give as a gift. At around 100 by 80 centimetres, they're a generous size without being unwieldy.

## How It's Made

The fleece is cut to size and the edges are carefully blanket-stitched by hand all the way around. This line of blanket stitches creates the anchor points for the crocheted border, which is then worked in double knit yarn in a complementary colour. The crochet adds just enough weight to help the blanket drape nicely, and gives it that lovely handmade finish you can spot from across the room.
