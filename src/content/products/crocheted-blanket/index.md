There's something wonderfully comforting about a granny square blanket, and each one of these is a proper labour of love. Measuring around 80 by 80 centimetres, they're just the right size for tucking around a little one in a pram or draping over the arm of a chair. The colours change with every blanket, so yours will be genuinely one of a kind.

## How It's Made

Each blanket starts from the centre and works outward in the classic granny square pattern, round by round. The yarn colours are chosen as I go, mixing and matching from my stash so no two blankets end up with the same combination. Once it reaches size, the edges are finished with a neat border round, and the whole thing gets a gentle wash and block to soften up before it goes to market.
